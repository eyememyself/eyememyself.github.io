1.3. On His Works

  • G. Chikermane, Reading Sri Aurobindo. Penguin Random House India Private Limited, 2022.
    • A new book on Sri Aurobindo and his works. It is written by experts, followers who largely agree with Sri Aurobindo's worldview. It includes overviews of his works which might be quite suitable for those keen on learning Sri Aurobindo's works as the authors themselves understand them.
